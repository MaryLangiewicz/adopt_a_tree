Gold mining causes major degradation of land and contaminates the region with heavy metals. Ten years ago, the local community began restoration efforts towards an abandoned gold mine, with the help of Carved Rock Fitness' NGO in the region. They built up the soil with vegetative matter. Once the soil was viable, they began planting native tree species. The trees attract butterflies, birds and small mammals from the surrounding forests.
